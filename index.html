Academia Ferrea

Time to switch things up - you in?

Academia Ferrea is the fabled forge of strength you suspected was too good to exist—a haven where discipline meets camaraderie, and excellence is the baseline, not the goal. It stands not merely as a gym, but as a testament to what training can become when shaped by purpose and community.

Our ambition is to transcend the ordinary—to refine the physical, uplift the spirit, and cultivate a culture not bound by trend but built to endure. This is not just a place to train; it is a place to become.

What sets us apart

We do not settle for less. Why don't you take a look below at some of the exlusive features that comes with being apart of the academy!

EXTENSIVE EQUIPMENT SELECTION

Our gym offers a broad selection of free weights and top-quality machines, all handpicked and approved by our experienced trainers.

Train with expert instructors in our Fighting Dojo, available four days a week. Outside of class times, the space is open for personal practice and self-training.

We got you covered! Pick up your bath towel when you arrive and drop it off when you leave. A small convenience for us, a big comfort for you.
